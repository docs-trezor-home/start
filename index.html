Trezor.io/Start

Welcome to Trezor.io/start, your ultimate guide to setting up your Trezor device. In this article, we will walk you through the process of getting started with Trezor Suite, Trezor Bridge, and Trezor Wallet.

Trezor Suite

Trezor Suite is the official desktop application for Trezor devices. It allows you to manage your cryptocurrencies, passwords, and other sensitive data in a secure and user-friendly way.

Trezor Bridge

Trezor Bridge is a web-based application that allows you to connect your Trezor device to your computer and manage your cryptocurrencies online.

Trezor Wallet

Trezor Wallet is a mobile application that allows you to manage your cryptocurrencies on-the-go. It is available for both Android and iOS devices.

By following these steps, you can easily set up your Trezor device and start managing your cryptocurrencies securely.
